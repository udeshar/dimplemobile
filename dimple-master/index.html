A safe space is where you can feel heard and understood without worry and judgement for your mental well being. Most of us fail to focus to understand and significance of mental health.We tend to take care of physical side of our health, be it

going to the gym

undertaking fancy diet

and the likes

Hovewer, sometime we don't even speak about our mental health and the complexities related to it. This often lead to frustrations and irritability, affecting our daily lives- be at home. School or at work. Hence, the need to work on this vital aspect.

We often tend to get frustrated, be it for not getting things right, for making the same mistakes or being in the same unhealthy pattern. an individual deciding to start working out and dieting from the next day but it never happens.

It's about how we treat ourselves during change. How we learned to love ourselves in the midst of struggle.

About me

I've always enjoyed connecting with people. Over time i realized this, and that drove my passion for working with. I quit my job as publicist, and started pursuing my Masters in Psychology from Indira Gandhi national Open University (IGNOU) and completed my Diploma in Advanced Psychology counseling from Don Bosco Prafulta (Mumbai).Show more

My enthusiasm to work with adolescents and young adults was further enhanced when i was provided an opportunity to conduct counseling sessions across Y.M.C.A (Andheri, Mumbai) and Mahim Church (Mumbai). Additionally, I conducted workshops across several schools and conducted activities regarding the importance of mental health in BMC (Brihun Mumbai Muncipality Corporation) schools with students and teachers. My approach to clients is by using transactional analysis and client centered therapy, my specialities include stress, relationship, self esteem, identity crisis, behaviour modifications.
